About Me

I am a pre-final year undergraduate student in the department of Mechanical Engineering with minor in Electrical Engineering (Power) at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, interested in the field of Dynamics, Mechanics of Solids, Automobile Design and Manufacturing.

I have been working in this field for the past three years through projects & competitions and wish to pursue a career in this field. I am a part of SAE team IITK Motorsports, and so far I have been involved in designing and manufacturing two vehicles, a Formula Student Vehicle and an All Terrain Vehicle. I have also been a part of the team designing a conceptual Electric Formula Student Vehicle. Currently, I am working on an All-terrain vehicle and also planning to participate in Shell Eco-Marathon whose primary target is to achieve the highest possible fuel efficiency.

I have a strong mechanical background and good academic skills. I am well versed with CAD and FEM as most of my previous work involved a lot of CAD and Simulation Softwares. I have completed coursework in Advanced Mechanics of Solids, Theory of Machines and Mechanisms, Dynamics, Energy Systems, Power Electronics, Manufacturing Science etc. Apart from my primary interests, I have also done project work pertaining to Image Processing. I also have a decent experience in coding using C, C++.
